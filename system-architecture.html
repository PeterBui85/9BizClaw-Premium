What Is 9BizClaw?

AI-powered business automation platform running as an Electron desktop app on the CEO's machine (Windows/Mac). It listens to Zalo customer messages, answers via LLM (via 9Router), manages long-term memory, runs scheduled automation (cron), and integrates with Google Workspace, Facebook, inventory, HR, and more. The CEO configures and controls; customers message the Zalo OA.
